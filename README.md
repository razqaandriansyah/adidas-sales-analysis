# Adidas Sales Profitability & Commercial Finance Analysis

## Overview

This project analyses Adidas US sales data from a commercial finance and FP&A perspective. The analysis focuses on profitability, operating margin, sales performance, and commercial trends across retailers, products, regions, cities, and sales channels.

The project combines data analytics with finance-style business interpretation to identify the key drivers of revenue and profitability performance.

---

## Objective

- Analyse Adidas sales and operating profit performance
- Identify the strongest and weakest commercial segments
- Compare profitability across products, retailers, and regions
- Evaluate sales channel performance
- Discover margin opportunities and operational risks
- Deliver finance-grade commercial insights and recommendations

---

## Key Insights

- The West region generated the highest revenue and operating profit contribution
- West Gear and Foot Locker emerged as the strongest retail partners
- Men’s Street Footwear delivered the strongest operating margin performance
- In-store sales remained the most profitable sales channel
- Online sales continue to grow but may pressure margins over time
- The Midwest region underperformed relative to other regions
- Revenue growth does not always translate into stronger profitability
